About Us

The vision of this project is to provide a sense of security and peace of mind to families and caregivers of individuals living with Alzheimer's disease. By leveraging technology in the form of a user-friendly website, we aim to address the challenges faced by those with Alzheimer's who may wander off due to memory loss. Our goal is to create a comprehensive platform where families can register their loved ones with Alzheimer's, providing essential details such as their name, address, and any health concerns that may affect their safety while traveling. Through the generation of a unique QR code, easily accessible through various means such as tattoos or ID cards, strangers encountering wandering individuals can quickly access vital information about them.
